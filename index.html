Sofies RPG

Project Overview

Sofies RPG is a 2D multiplayer RPG inspired by classic side-scrolling MMORPGs while expanding into survival systems, housing, dungeon progression and persistent online gameplay.


The project focuses heavily on gameplay architecture and multiplayer systems developed entirely inside MapleStory Worlds.
